AfriFoundry's Methodology

How D.R.E.A.M. Works

The validation framework at the core of AfriFoundry's infrastructure ‚Äî and the engine behind AfriFoundry AI. Five stages that turn raw ideas into market-tested solutions.

D
Discover

Surface the real opportunity. Uncover what the market actually needs ‚Äî not what founders assume it needs.

R
Refine

Strip the idea to its core. Remove assumptions that don't survive contact with real market data.

E
Ethics

Validate that the solution respects the community it serves. No extraction. No exploitation.

A
Analyze

Run the numbers against real market data. Competition, pricing, demand, margin. Ground truth.

M
Manifest

Turn a validated idea into a community-ready launch. With confidence, not hope.

AfriFoundry AI's Engine

The TEMIS Framework

TEMIS is the scoring engine that runs silently behind every AfriFoundry AI conversation. You experience it as a thinking partner asking the right questions. The full breakdown is revealed in your validation report.

T
Technical

Can this actually be built given Kenya's infrastructure realities? Power, internet, logistics.

E
Economic

Do the numbers work in real African conditions? Margins, pricing power, end-of-month behaviour.

M
Maintenance

Can this be sustained long-term? Supply chain stability, talent, ongoing operational costs.

I
Infrastructure

Does local infrastructure support this? Roads, power grid, mobile money, last-mile delivery.

S
Systemic

Does it fit the African ecosystem? Culture, trust, regulation, community dynamics.

How TEMIS works

TEMIS scores update silently during every conversation ‚Äî guiding which assumptions AfriFoundry AI challenges next. You never see percentages. You just experience sharper, more relevant questions.
