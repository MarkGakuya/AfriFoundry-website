Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026  ·  AfriFoundry

Short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. Your business ideas are yours — we never share or claim them. You can delete your data any time.

1. Who We Are

AfriFoundry operates AfriFoundry AI at ai.afrifoundry.com — a thinking partner for African entrepreneurs — and the company website at afrifoundry.com.

Questions? Email support@afrifoundry.com

2. What We Collect

When you register: your name, email, and password (stored as a secure hash — never plain text). Optionally your organisation and location if you choose to share them.

During use: messages you send to AfriFoundry AI and the AI's responses. Basic usage counts to understand how the product is being used.

We do not collect payment information. No paid plans currently exist.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To run your account and keep you logged in
  • To provide your conversation history across sessions
  • To improve AfriFoundry AI — only using anonymised, aggregated patterns, never your specific idea
  • To communicate service updates when needed

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build ad profiles. We do not sell your data.

4. Your Ideas Are Protected

Your specific business idea is never shared with any third party in identifiable form. AfriFoundry does not use your idea to build competing products. Any internal analysis is fully anonymised before we look at anything.

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Database access is restricted to AfriFoundry's backend only.

AfriFoundry is an early-stage product. We take security seriously — no system is perfect. If you spot an issue, email support@afrifoundry.com immediately.

6. Third Parties

AfriFoundry does not sell your personal data. To operate the service, conversation content is processed by the AI providers powering AfriFoundry AI. We share only what's necessary to generate responses — we never send your name or account details to any AI provider.

We will disclose data if required by Kenyan law or a valid legal process, and will notify you unless prohibited.

7. Your Rights

You can access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Email support@afrifoundry.com with subject "Data Request" — we'll respond within 7 business days. Deleting your account removes all your data within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Session cookies only — to keep you logged in. No advertising cookies, no tracking pixels, no third-party analytics.

9. Updates

We may update this policy as the product grows. Registered users will be notified by email when we make material changes. Continued use means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Email: support@afrifoundry.com
